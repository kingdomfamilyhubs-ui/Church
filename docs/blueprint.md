# **App Name**: Growing In Faith Global Church

## Core Features:

- Navigation & Core Pages: Implement essential church pages such as 'About Us', 'Beliefs', and 'Contact Info' with clear navigation.
- Event Calendar & Listings: Display upcoming church events, schedules, and details in an easily browsable format.
- Sermon Library & Player: Provide a section for visitors to listen to or watch past sermons and messages, with integrated media playback.
- Online Giving Link: A clear call to action and secure link or integration for online donations and giving.
- Prayer Request & Contact Form: Enable visitors to submit prayer requests or general inquiries through a user-friendly contact form.
- AI Scripture Insight Tool: An AI tool that provides a brief historical, theological, and contextual background for specific Bible verses referenced in sermons or highlighted on the site.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Violet-Blue (#3D2999) to convey spiritual depth and authority, designed to stand out on a dark background.
- Background color: Very dark Grey-Violet (#18141A), providing a serene and focused backdrop that allows the primary and accent colors to shine.
- Accent color: Lighter, clear Blue (#6BA9DB), offering a sense of openness and guidance, chosen as an analogous hue to the primary for visual harmony and contrast.
- Headline and body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, highly legible, and neutral aesthetic, ensuring clear communication of all content.
- Use clean, contemporary icons with subtle religious or spiritual motifs, maintaining clarity and reverence across all interactive elements and content categories.
- A clean, content-centric layout inspired by traditional church website structures, featuring a prominent hero section, clearly defined content blocks for events and sermons, and an intuitive top-level navigation, with a strong focus on responsiveness for mobile viewing.
- Subtle, fluid animations for page transitions and element interactions, such as gentle fades for content loading and understated hover effects on navigational items, designed to enhance user engagement without distraction.
